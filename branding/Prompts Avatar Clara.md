# PROMPTS — AVATAR OFICIAL CLARA v2.0

> Prompts prontos para geração do avatar da influencer IA Clara.
> Alinhados com Brand Engine v2 — paleta azul escuro / amarelo / branco / preto suave.
> Data de criação: 2026-05-13

---

## OBJETIVO

Gerar 5 variações do avatar oficial da Clara para uso em:
- Foto de perfil (Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, WhatsApp)
- Thumbnails de stories e reels
- Cards de produto com presença da Clara
- Banners de página e destaque de stories

---

## IDENTIDADE FIXA (âncora de consistência)

Todos os prompts compartilham estes elementos imutáveis:

| Elemento | Definição |
|----------|-----------|
| Etnia | Brasileira, traços miscigenados (morena clara a parda) |
| Cabelo | Castanho escuro, liso ou levemente ondulado |
| Idade aparente | 27–33 anos |
| Estilo | Casual premium — blusa simples, sem ostentação |
| Tom | Confiante, inteligente, acessível — não glamourosa |
| Cores proibidas | Vermelho, laranja, rosa, neon |
| Paleta aprovada | Azul escuro `#0D1B4B`, amarelo `#FFD600`, branco, cinza neutro |

---

## VARIAÇÃO 1 — IDENTIDADE PRINCIPAL

**Uso:** foto de perfil oficial, avatar fixo da marca

```
Photorealistic portrait of a Brazilian woman, 28-32 years old, 
mixed ethnicity (morena clara), straight dark brown hair, 
warm and confident smile, direct eye contact with camera. 
Wearing a clean white blouse, minimalist style. 
Solid deep navy blue background (#0D1B4B). 
Soft diffused frontal studio lighting, no harsh shadows. 
Headshot framing, centered composition. 
Casual premium aesthetic — intelligent, approachable, trustworthy. 
No heavy makeup, no jewelry, no glamour. 
Not a model pose. Real and warm. High resolution, 4K, sharp focus.
Negative prompt: red clothing, neon colors, heavy makeup, 
artificial smile, influencer pose, glamorous, infantile, studio infinity background.
```

## VARIAÇÃO 2 — ACOLHEDORA / COMUNIDADE

**Uso:** posts de engajamento, stories de curadoria semanal, carrossel de apresentação

```
Photorealistic portrait of a Brazilian woman, 28-32 years old, 
mixed ethnicity (parda), straight dark brown hair loosely framed, 
genuine warm smile, slightly turned body at 3/4 angle toward camera. 
Wearing a simple navy blue blouse, casual and clean. 
Clean white wall background with soft natural shadow. 
Soft natural window light from the left side, cool-neutral temperature. 
Bust framing, relaxed posture — not posed. 
Feels like someone you know, not a celebrity. 
Intelligent and caring expression. High resolution, 4K.
Negative prompt: red, orange, pink palette, neon, heavy makeup, 
stiff pose, over-styled, glamorous, busy background.
```

## VARIAÇÃO 3 — AUTORIDADE / ESPECIALISTA

**Uso:** posts de curadoria premium, thumbnails de reels explicativos, banners da página

```
Photorealistic portrait of a Brazilian woman, 28-32 years old, 
mixed ethnicity, dark brown straight hair, subtle confident smile, 
direct and calm gaze — the look of someone who knows what they're talking about. 
Wearing a structured navy blue casual blazer over a white inner blouse. 
Background: deep navy blue gradient, dark to slightly lighter at top. 
Cool soft studio lighting, slight edge light from left. 
Centered bust framing, upright posture, composed. 
Premium and modern feel — like a trusted media curator, not a salesperson.
```

## VARIAÇÃO 4 — DESCOBERTA / MOMENTO REAL

**Uso:** stories de achado, posts de urgência real, reels de apresentação de produto

```
Photorealistic photo of a Brazilian woman, 28-32 years old, 
mixed ethnicity, dark brown straight hair, natural expression of genuine interest — 
looking at a smartphone screen with subtle excitement, not exaggerated. 
Wearing a beige or off-white casual blouse. 
Background: slightly blurred modern home interior — clean minimalist living room, 
neutral tones (white, light grey), no clutter. 
Soft warm-neutral natural daylight from a window to the right. 
Mid-shot framing, relaxed and candid — as if caught in a real moment. 
The phone is held naturally, not displayed toward camera. 
Authentic, relatable, casual premium feel. High resolution, 4K.
Negative prompt: red, neon, posed hands, studio background, 
glamour, heavy filter, dramatic lighting, cluttered space.
```

## VARIAÇÃO 5 — DINÂMICA / STORIES

**Uso:** thumbnail de stories, avatar animado, capa de destaque do Instagram

```
Photorealistic close-up portrait of a Brazilian woman, 28-32 years old, 
mixed ethnicity, dark brown straight hair, animated and engaged expression — 
sharing something she genuinely finds impressive, slight eyebrow raise, 
real smile, not exaggerated. 
Wearing a navy or dark blue fitted casual top. 
Background: deep navy blue (#0D1B4B) with a subtle thin yellow (#FFD600) 
geometric accent line at one edge — minimal brand detail. 
Soft ring light, clean and modern. 
Tight bust framing, dynamic and present — like pausing a video to look at you. 
High energy but controlled — 7/10, not 10/10. High resolution, 4K.
Negative prompt: red, orange, pink, heavy jewelry, exaggerated expression, 
over-acting, infantile, generic influencer, glossy magazine aesthetic.
```

## CRITÉRIOS DE ESCOLHA DO AVATAR FINAL

Ao gerar as imagens, escolher a que melhor atender:

- [ ] Passa como uma pessoa real brasileira — não parece IA óbvia
- [ ] Transmite confiança sem distância — acessível, não celebridade
- [ ] Lê-se bem em 50×50px (tamanho de avatar mobile)
- [ ] Paleta alinhada: fundo azul escuro ou branco, sem vermelho
- [ ] Expressão natural — não pose de modelo nem sorriso artificial
- [ ] Não parece influencer genérica de afiliado
